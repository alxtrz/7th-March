About Us

"Sports teaches you to understand the meaning of a team. You need to be able to work with everybody; you don't have to be their best friend. You can experience the fun of competition and driving toward a common goal without pushing to bond in some major way with each individual on a project."

With this ambition of creating a platform where people can not only come to showcase their talent, but also can represent their team and connect with players and share everyone's common passion for sports, we started Aarambh.

AARAMBH is our college's immensely popular annual inter college sports fest, filled with energy, entertainment and fun. Every year, hundreds of college all around North India participate in various sports events held under our fest and be a part of this spectacular 3 day event. There's no better way to loosen up than to play together and to learn from your experiences, either success or failure.

We has a footfall of over 3000 students. They participate in various games namely, basketball, cricket, badminton, table tennis, chess and carrom.

Come join us and have an amazing time with remarkable chief guests, cooperative team, and quality officials.
